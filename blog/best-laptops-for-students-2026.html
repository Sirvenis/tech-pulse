What Students Need

  • Battery Life: All-day battery (8+ hours) for classes, library, and coffee shops

  • Performance: Enough power for web browsing, documents, video calls, and light creative work
  • Durability: Solid build quality to survive student life
  • Value: Good performance per dollar — students are on a budget
