What to Look For

  • Resolution: 1080p (budget), 1440p (sweet spot), 4K (premium), ultrawide (immersive)
  • Refresh Rate: 144Hz minimum for gaming, 240Hz+ for competitive, 360Hz for esports
  • Panel Type: IPS for color accuracy, VA for contrast, OLED for best overall
  • Response Time: 1ms GtG or less for competitive gaming
  • Adaptive Sync: G-Sync (NVIDIA) or FreeSync (AMD) to eliminate screen tearing
