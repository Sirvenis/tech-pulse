What to Look For

  • Performance: Latest chipset (Snapdragon 8 Elite, Apple A19 Pro, Tensor G5) for speed and efficiency
  • Camera: Main sensor quality, ultrawide, telephoto, and computational photography capabilities
  • Display: OLED with 120Hz+ refresh rate, high brightness for outdoor use
  • Battery: All-day battery life with fast charging support (wired and wireless)
  • Software Updates: 5-7 years of OS and security updates
