Choosing Your Ecosystem

Before buying devices, decide on your ecosystem:

  • Amazon Alexa: Widest device compatibility, best for voice control. Choose Echo devices.
  • Google Home: Best integration with Android and Google services. Choose Nest devices.
  • Apple HomeKit: Best privacy and security, seamless with Apple devices. Choose HomeKit-compatible devices.
  • Matter: The new universal standard. Works across all ecosystems. Look for "Works with Matter" on new devices.
