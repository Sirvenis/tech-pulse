Best USB-C Chargers 2026

Published: April 2026 | Reading time: 8 min

With USB-C now the universal charging standard (thanks to EU regulations and Apple's switch), one charger can power your laptop, phone, tablet, earbuds, and more. GaN technology has made chargers smaller and more powerful than ever. Here are our top picks for 2026.

What to Look For

  • Wattage: 30W for phones, 65W for ultrabooks, 100W+ for gaming laptops and MacBook Pro
  • Ports: Single-port is smallest, multi-port lets you charge multiple devices simultaneously
  • GaN Technology: Gallium Nitride chargers are smaller and more efficient than silicon
  • PD (Power Delivery): Ensure the charger supports USB Power Delivery for fast laptop charging
  • Size & Weight: GaN chargers are now pocket-sized at 65W+

Charging Speed Guide

  • 30W: Fast charges all phones, charges tablets slowly, trickle charges laptops
  • 65W: Charges ultrabooks (MacBook Air, Dell XPS) at full speed, fast charges phones
  • 100W: Charges MacBook Pro 14" and most laptops at full speed
  • 140W+: Required for MacBook Pro 16" and gaming laptops at full speed
