Published: April 2026 | Reading time: 9 min

Wireless earbuds have come a long way. In 2026, you can get excellent noise cancellation, great sound, and all-day comfort for under $100. But with dozens of options, which ones are worth your money? We tested the top earbuds to find the best picks for every need.

What to Look For

  • Active Noise Cancellation (ANC): How well does it block ambient noise? Transparency mode quality?
  • Sound Quality: Clarity, bass response, soundstage, and tuning quality
  • Comfort & Fit: Lightweight design, secure fit, multiple ear tip sizes
  • Battery Life: 6+ hours per charge, 24+ hours with case
  • Call Quality: Microphone clarity, wind noise reduction, multi-mic setup
